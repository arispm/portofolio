About Me

I am an architect with over 2 years of experience, specializing in designs that emphasize simplicity and beauty through minimalistic forms. My design philosophy is rooted in the principles of modern minimalism, where every element of the building is crafted to create functional, elegant, and harmonious spaces without unnecessary embellishments.

I believe that simple design can enhance the quality of life by providing comfort and tranquility. Additionally, I am a student at the 17 Agustus 1945 University Surabaya (Untag), continuously developing my knowledge and skills to produce high-quality architectural works that are relevant to the times.
